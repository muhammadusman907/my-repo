Employers are required to provide employees with safe access to scaffolds. While working on scaffolding, workers are most vulnerable to falls while ascending and descending scaffolds. Furthermore, scaffold erectors and dismantlers face additional hazards due to the incomplete nature of scaffolding during the construction and dismantling of these structures.

Let us first discuss the access requirements of workers who work on scaffolds. We will then focus on the access requirements relating to scaffold erectors and dismantlers.
